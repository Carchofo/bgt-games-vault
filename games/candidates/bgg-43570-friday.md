# Friday (2011)

## Solo mode
Friday is a solo-only deck-builder where you help Robinson Crusoe survive. Each turn you fight hazard cards using your deck; win fights to improve your deck, lose to add weak fear cards. Three difficulty levels via aging deck. Game ends when you defeat the two pirate ships.

## What BGT would implement
- Tab Solo: hazard fight tracker — current fight strength, free cards drawn
- Tab Scoring: life points remaining + final score
- Tab Rules: turn structure reference
- Tab Setup: deck preparation by difficulty

## Feasibility
**High.** Simplest solo logic of all candidates. Life counter + deck quality tracker. Very achievable.
