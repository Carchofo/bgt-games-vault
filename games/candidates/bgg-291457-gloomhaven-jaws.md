# Gloomhaven: Jaws of the Lion (2020)

## Solo mode
1 player controls 2 characters through dungeon scenarios. Monster AI driven by ability card decks. Tracking monster HP, conditions (stun/poison/immobilize), and initiative would be the core app value.

## What BGT would implement
- Tab Solo: monster HP + condition tracker per room
- Tab Rules: monster AI reference
- Tab Setup: scenario setup guide

## Feasibility
**Low.** Full dungeon tracker very complex. Monster HP widget is achievable minimum.
