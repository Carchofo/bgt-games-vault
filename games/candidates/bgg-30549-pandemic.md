# Pandemic (2008)

## Solo mode
1 player controls all roles simultaneously. Cure 4 diseases before outbreaks/deck runs out. App would track infection rate, outbreak counter (0-8), and role ability reference.

## What BGT would implement
- Tab Solo: outbreak counter + infection rate tracker
- Tab Rules: role abilities reference
- Tab Setup: initial infection setup guide

## Feasibility
**Medium.** Simple counters. Low novelty — many Pandemic apps exist already.
