# Coimbra (2018)

## Solo mode
Fan-made "Bot de interferencia" by Dave Smith (skybowl, BGG). Bot rolls dice for color/zone, uses a 7th spare die for castle. Bot always goes first. No pilgrimages. Player gets 1 crown for being 2nd.
